Docs Preparation
(1). Work with CS and TS agile teams to produce and finalize user-stories and acceptance criteria required for the campaign production cycle and definition of done. This includes defining and providing documentation for any additional project informaton, such as supplements for technical details, login credentials, staging environments and the like. Rely on documentation when needed;

(2). Create/maintain/facilitate knowledge base of each client, based on previous tests, so it can be easily accessed and used by each team member during refinement and production to assist in working on new campaigns.

(3). Perform site and test pages analysis to assist Business Analyst in user-stories completion \ refinement, and additional use-cases discovery and description.

(4). Proactive analysis of the test scope to define possible risks.

(5). Suggest possible solutions, and demonstrate their pros&cons, subsantial analysis of ther above in the light of product limitations, knowledge of possible and acceptable workarounds.

(6). Create and maintain knowledge base of each client based on previous tests so it can be easily accessed and used by each team member to work on new campaigns, and facilitate communications on a specific client.

Core Competencies: Technical Analyst
Aptitude Evaluation by Core Competencies
Core Competencies
Category
Communication

(Written)

1. Writes well-balanced documents, e-mails, presentations.

(Verbal)

1. Proactively raises issues.

2. Maintains meeting minutes.

3. Actively questions and clarifies understanding, receives and provides constructive feedback.

4. Strong & confident presenter, effectively conveys information to key stakeholders.
Analytical Skills

1. Foresees problems and takes preventive measures. Breaks down problems into smaller components. Synthesizes ideas towards solutions.

2. Substantiates solutions with evidence and arguments. Weighs all the pros and cons.

Detects consistent patterns to work out unified approach to problem solving. Analyses dependencies and co-dependencies.

3. Exhibits well-structured report writing skills.
Problem Solving

1. Suggests workable methods of problem-solving and chooses the best.

2. Demonstrates resourcefulness: locates useful information for resolving problems.

3. Motivates others to resolve problems.
Teamwork

1. Puts team goals above individual needs.

2. Teams up with others to reach common goals.

3. Inspires all team members to be useful for company by own example.
Time and Task Management

1. Compares the task requirements against possibility / time to resolve.

2. Consults / collaborates with involved teamplayes in a timely manner.

3. Maintains balance between performance rate and quality of work.
Attitude

(Responsibility / Reaching Goals)

1. Demonstrates self-standing problem resolution and availability to help others.

(Initiative / Self-improvement)

1. Learns and applies new approaches.

2. Has a positive attitude to feedback.

3. Shares experience by keeping database of acceptable methods and necessary skills in working with specific clients, usual pitfalls and workarounds.

(6) Works as a communications facilitator and liaison between CS and TS teams, the client's side, if necessary. 

(7) Understands Maxymiser system components interconnection, 'Publish' Process and dependencies;

R&D
Category TA
Contribution

(1). Learns known bug-list form common practices on a given Client.Follows team's knowledge base.

(2).Participates in discussions of known bugs and code optimization.

(3). Creates entries to bug lists \ knowledge db for others.
